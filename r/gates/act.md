# act

**Reference URL:** `cgp:/r/gates/act.md`

Decision gate that proceeds and acts on the data. When a trigger under `act` fires, the boundary crossing succeeds; the trigger's handler payload describes the action to take.

One of three decision gates. See also: `cgp:/r/gates/halt.md`, `cgp:/r/gates/ask.md`.
