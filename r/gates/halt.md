# halt

**Reference URL:** `cgp:/r/gates/halt.md`

Decision gate that blocks the in-flight action. When a trigger under `halt` fires, the boundary crossing is rejected; downstream work does not proceed until the condition is resolved.

One of three decision gates. See also: `cgp:/r/gates/ask.md`, `cgp:/r/gates/act.md`.
